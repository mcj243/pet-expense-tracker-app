Quick Guide & Data Key

Expense Tracker Key

  • **Date:** The date the expense occurred.
  • **Category:** Select the relevant type. **Food/Treats** and **Vet Bills** are key categories.
  • **Cost (Â£):** Enter the amount **without** any currency symbol. (e.g., `25.50`)
  • **Description:** Use this for details (e.g., `New Bag of Kibble`, `Annual Booster Shot`).
  • *Dashboard Calculation:* The Dashboard automatically **sums up all costs** to give you a yearly total.

Health Log Key

  • **Date:** The date the treatment or appointment happened.
  • **Type:** Choose the type of event (e.g., Vaccination).
  • **Next Due:** **Crucial for reminders!** Input the date the next treatment is due. *If not applicable, leave blank.*
  • **Notes:** Record details like product name, specific medicine, or weight at the time of the visit.
  • *Dashboard Calculation:* The Dashboard finds the **soonest upcoming date** from this column to remind you.

**Data Tip:** Your data is saved automatically in the secure cloud. You can access it on any device.
